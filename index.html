About Me


Welcome all to my landing page! My name is Josh Eshleman. I am a 28 year old aspiring developer. I spent years 19-28 of my life working in Distribution and Logistics for Ollie's Bargain Outlet Inc. I started with Ollie's in my home town of Harrisburg, PA a year after graduating high school. I continued to further my career within the company, until an opportunity to move and become one of five people selected to start up the company's second Distribution Center presented itself. Opening in Commerce, GA in 2014, this 1 million square foot Distribution Center rapidly grew from 5-300 employees. I was the Supervisor of my own department, as well as 30+ associates, from March, 2014 - January, 2019, and gained an expert array of skills in regards to people management.


I was a very successful supervisor and was offered promotional opportunities on multiple occasions however, I respectfully declined these positions, as I was considering a change in career paths. This change was made possible when my wife graduated from Mercer University College of Pharmacy and began a career of her own. I began seeking other lifelong interests and taught myself some basic coding skills. Realizing a more professional learning environment and certification would likeley be required to turn coding into a profession, I sought out and found Georiga Tech's Coding Bootcamp program, immediately enrolled, and am currently a student of.

Thank you for taking the time to check out my story! Check the links in the header for contact info and view my work on the portfolio page!
